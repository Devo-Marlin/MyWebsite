Miata Build Vlog

Day 1 – Tear Down Begins

Today I started the Miata build! I stripped off the bumpers, fenders, and interior carpet to prep for rust repair and weight savings. Found a few spots that need patching, but overall, the frame is in great shape.

Day 2 – Chassis Prep & Paint

Cleaned up surface rust, sprayed rust preventative, and hit the engine bay with satin black. It’s already looking way cleaner. Next step is seam sealer and prepping for suspension install.

Day 3 – Suspension Mockup

Installed coilovers and test-fit control arms. Seeing it dropped to track height makes it feel real. Can’t wait to corner balance and align. Still working on getting the right wheels and tires.
